SIT102 Introduction to Programming  P10 Learning Summary

Learning Summary

Demonstrate you have achieved the unit learning outcomes.

Show us that you can:

    · Demonstrate that you have met the Unit Learning Outcomes (ULO) of the
         unit.

    · Highlight where and how you meet the criteria for your target grade.
    · Demonstrate how you have grown as an IT professional, highlighting the

         professional characteristics you have developed and how you believe
         these will help you succeed in the future.
    · Reflect on your learning journey, and not just summarise. You could use
         the "What? So What? Now What?" critical reflection model as a guide.

Do the following:

         1. Download and complete the Learning Summary Template.
         2. Upload to the task for feedback.
         3. Create your portfolio using the Portfolio creation tool in OnTrack.

Tip: Really take the time to reflect on your trimester

This task is in preparation for your Final Portfolio. We are looking for sincere
reflections here. Rushed or insincere reflections do not provide you any value ­
instead, take the time to really reflect on your journey this trimester. This unit is
just the beginning of a much larger journey, so best to get off to the right start.
